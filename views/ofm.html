The ObserverFood Monthly

The Project

The Observer Food Monthly collates the best of the best in all things culinary. This project was created to house the 2018 list of the world’s top 50 in people, places, food and drink.

I designed and built a custom, immersive template which provided a series of full-bleed image headers, large secondary images and graphical pull-quotes for the editors to structure their content around. Launched shortly after the Guardian’s redesign, I made specific use of the new in-house visual style, with an emphasis on line, structural layout, bold palette and highlighting.
