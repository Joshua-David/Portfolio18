Condé NastDigital

The Project

I worked for Condé Nast Digital when I first moved to London, giving me creative access to a remarkable and iconic range of brands including Vogue, Wired, GQ and Tattler. I worked on a team that created bespoke websites for external brands which would sit within one of Condé Nast’s in-house sites.

The goal was always to create a beautiful, high-spec piece of digital design that would balance the external and internal brands needs. Projects often involved bespoke photoshoots and editorial, I was responsible for digital direction and design. This is a small selection of some of the work I produced for them.
